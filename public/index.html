2. Host (Creator)

Hosts earn primarily through Live Streaming. Your main income is a 70% share of Virtual Gifts received from viewers. Hosts earn extra rewards by participating in platform events, reaching top regional rankings, and completing daily streaming tasks.

Coin Sellers operate as verified resellers, generating profit by acquiring Poppo Coins at competitive bulk rates (via E-pay, USDT, Binance) and reselling them to other users at a favorable margin. You can also exchange app points for coins at excellent rates to sell.
